There are so many great movies out there to be seen!

If this is the first time you are using the site, the collection and playlists are populated with sample movies. Once you start gathering your own collection, the sample data will clear.

Start by searching for a movie and adding it to your collection. Once in your collection you can have quick access through the "All Movies" search. There are also 4 playlists that you can use - Action, Drama, Horror and Fantasy. When you are viewing a movie, you can add that movie to a playlist.
